Jotiba is a well known deity of whole Maharashtra. It is the Kul-Dev of most of people from Maharashtra.
Once a ling of Jotiba was displaced. It was believed that the ling would take its own place with a touch of the sacred & pious Raja or Sardar. Sardar of Nipani (Nipanikar Sardar) by this time came to know that Maharaj is not an ordinary cow-boy. But a person having a super natural power. He equated him to omnipotent God. He naturally went to Halsiddhanath to consult whether he should gofer setting the ‘Ling’ or not. Nath Maharaj gave him a green signal to go but cautioned him not to get garlanded or join the procession of victory.

He went & with his pious touches the ‘Ling” found its original place. Nath Maharaj perceived this well in advance, entered into the Samadhi & got the door shut. While entering in the Samadhi he gave his belongings to his devotees.
