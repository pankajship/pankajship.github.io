  Kurli is a small village from Chikodi Taluka in Belgum District. It is hardly 3 to 4 kilometers from Appachiwadi. Appachiwadi is a small Wadi under Kurli village.

Halsiddhanath first came to Kurli from “Chikodi” when he came to Chikotra Chikhali. He found that the river Chikotra was flowing fast & deep in between Chikhali & in the evening. The boatman who used to take & bring people from one bank to another had called off the day. Halsiddhanath had to stay at Chikhali over night. Now a temple of Halsiddhanath is built  near the river in the memory of his stay.

In early morning, Halsiddhanath came near the river but the boatman was not there so early. He threw his ‘Ghongada’ in the river plain & sat on it & went to Kurli. People hearing this, were very much astonished. He stayed at Kurli. He then went to Appachiwadi. Still small village he made Appachiwadi his “Karmabhumi”. He used to perform Penance, sitting, the cave like place. The door of the place is so small that one has to be horizontal to enter into in place. He has won the trust of people very soon. He was always referred as an arbitrated in village disputes. He used to heal the diseases of poor people with his power of quantum healing. He became with his philatropical nature cynosure of eyes. People started worshiping him as incarnation of God.

Now it was time for taking ‘Samadhi’ just like Dnyaneshwar. He took “Sanjeevan Samadhi”. The concept of Sanjeevan Samadhi is still opeque.
